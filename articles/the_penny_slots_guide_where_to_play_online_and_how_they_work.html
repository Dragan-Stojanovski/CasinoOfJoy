2. Pay Attention to the RTP

Penny slots are a lot of fun, but depending on the return to player percentage, you can gain more for your money by betting less frequently on a higher-priced slot machine. This is because higher-priced slot machines typically have a greater maximum bet.

A slot machine's Return to Player (RTP) percentage reveals how much money a player may reasonably expect to lose if they repeatedly play the machine over an extended period. Suppose the return to player percentage (RTP) of a penny slot machine is less than 90%. In that case, playing at that machine may not be as profitable as playing at a machine with a higher RTP that accepts larger bets.

You will, of course, require a larger bankroll in order to play the slot machine with the bigger possible payout for the same amount of time. Because of this, you need to decide how much weight to give to the game's ability to entertain you vs how much weight to give to the game's ability to affect your bankroll.

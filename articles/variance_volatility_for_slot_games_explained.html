Variance & Volatility for Slot Games Explained

19.September.2022

The results of games played on slot machines are notoriously hard to anticipate. You might have a session in which you win several hundred dollars. Then immediately after that, you might have a session in which you lose the same amount in a little over an hour. Slot machines provide significantly less predictable results than the more conventional card and table games like blackjack and baccarat.

The probability of success in these games is greater than fifty per cent. Slot machines have higher volatility and variance than other casino games do, as compared to those other games. But what exactly do these terms mean, and in what contexts might one make use of them concerning slot machines? Continue reading to learn more about volatility and variance and how these factors affect your chances of winning.

What Is Variance?

A statistical measure of the dispersion of findings over time is referred to as the variance. To put it another way, it is a measurement of the extent to which individual results can vary from the average. It is important to note that variance is not the same thing as standard deviation, which is the square root of variance. Standard deviation is a more accurate representation of the average dispersion of outcomes. It is not a fixed statistic like standard variation but rather a measurement of how open to change the outcomes are. Standard variation is a fixed statistic.

In contrast to Hawaii, Michigan is more likely to see significant swings in the weather. Because of this, Michigan experiences a wider range of temperatures throughout the year. One more of this phrase's appealing qualities is that it is not constrained to a particular amount of time, such as a week or a month. Specifically, it takes into account the likelihood of alterations occurring over time.


What Is Volatility?

Volatility, a measure of the dispersion of outcomes, is somewhat comparable to variance in its application. To put that in perspective, this one has a firm due date connected to it. When one considers the degree to which sales of automobiles change from one week to the next within the same calendar month, one might say that automobile sales are volatile. On the other hand, variance does not mandate that data be gathered over the course of a single month. The speed and the size of changes in data can be accurately measured with the help of a useful indicator called volatility. In the short term, it is beneficial because it helps determine the type of risk involved.


How Do Variance and Volatility Affect Slots Results?

Slot machines are the most unpredictable and volatile casino games available, especially compared to other options. Due to the large disparity between first and second place, the payouts are not as consistent as those of other games. Volatility becomes a significant consideration while playing slots because the results of any given session of play can be wholly or entirely arbitrary. It all depends on the game you play, but the prize may easily reach six figures. On the other hand, it's possible that you could lose a thousand dollars in a couple of hours.

A number of gamblers enjoy the excitement that comes with an element of surprise. That is a strong motivation for them to continue playing the slot machines in the expectation that they will hit it big. Because of the inherent randomness of the game, playing slot machines can be considered high risk over time. Even though they are intended to deliver a particular RTP, they still deliver dramatically diverse results for each player despite the fact that they are designed to do so. Some slot machines may require millions of spins before reaching the RTP advertised for them. They are capable of exhibiting a great degree of unpredictability even throughout very long periods.

Due to the tremendous volatility and unpredictability of slot machine games, it is infamously difficult to keep track of your bankroll while playing them. Even if you have a strategy, it is still a significant possibility that your plan for managing your bankroll would stray from the path you had meant for it to take. You have to be mentally prepared to accept the element of chance if you want to play slot machines to the fullest extent possible. If you are willing to acknowledge this limitation, you should have a lot of fun playing these games.


High vs Low Slots Variance and Volatility

There are slot machines that have a low variance, as well as ones that have high volatility. Acquiring the knowledge necessary to differentiate between slots with high and low variance will allow you to select the most suitable game for your playing style and bankroll. There are various games in which the volatility rating is included in the pay table.

For example, the volatility of Pragmatic Play slots is graded from 1 to 5 volts, with 5 volts being the riskiest play possible. For whatever reason, I've never come across a slot machine that exhibited the kind of volatility that was advertised for it. However, the volatility score can give you an approximation of the fluctuations you should anticipate. There is no rule that says slot machines have to give these functions. Instead, when trying to predict future volatility and dispersion, you need to consider various elements that are not under your control.

Volatility and variance cannot be determined with any degree of precision using merely this information as the basis for the analysis. Nonetheless, they do offer a method for determining which machines are more random than others, so you can use them to your advantage. It bears repeating that the terms "variation" and "volatility" are not interchangeable. The first gives a notion of how things will roll out over time. In contrast, the second provides quantitative information on how things will progress over a certain length of time. On the other hand, slot machines are not very accurate in the evaluations that they provide, so you won't get a clear picture of how each of these aspects plays out in each game. The volatility rating of each slot machine must be used as a proxy for the variance of that machine whenever one is available.


Will Understanding These Terms Help You Win in Slots?

When you play slots, the most effective strategy for managing your bankroll is to understand the games' volatility and unpredictability. If you have a low bankroll and want it to last as long as possible, you should play games with a modest amount of volatility and variance. You generally don't want to get involved in the play of a game that has a high degree of volatility and unpredictability.

Extreme volatility pushes the stakes higher, increasing the likelihood that you may run out of money in a short amount of time. However, there are aspects of high-volatility slots that appeal to players. You might be interested in playing games that have huge jackpots and a wide variety of bonuses. It is also crucial to take into consideration the potential influence that an unpredictable game could have on your bankroll. Unpredictable games have a chance of producing life-altering jackpot wins every once in a while.
